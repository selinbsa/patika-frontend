Most Friendly Dogs

Dogs have always been known as loyal and loving companions, but some breeds stand out with their especially friendly nature. Golden Retrievers, Labradors, and Beagles are among the top choices for families and first-time dog owners. These breeds are gentle, affectionate, and usually get along well with children and other pets. Whether you're looking for a playful buddy or a calm cuddle partner, these friendly breeds are sure to warm your heart.
